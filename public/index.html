Building Tech and Community in Baltimore City

We meet 6:30 PM every Thursday at The Impact Hub 10 E North Ave Baltimore, MD 21202.

We operate four different nights on a rotating schedule:

Speaker Night

On speaker night we feature either guest speakers or our very own members who want to present on a particular topic.

Social Night

Socializing, networking, and getting to know your peers is always a part of the successful professional development process!

Project Night

On project nights, we dive head first into the projects in which we are most interested.

Study Group Night

Study group night is an opportunity for the group to collaborate on projects or share ideas.
